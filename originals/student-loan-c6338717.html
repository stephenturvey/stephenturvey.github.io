Your student loan for BA (Honours) Business Studies

Apply for your 2014/15 student loan

Complete an online application

Apply online with . You'll create an account, and will be given a unique Customer Reference Number (CRN). Keep a record of your CRN, so that you can complete your Open University module registration.

Complete an online application

Note: depending on your circumstances when applying online to SFE you may still need to submit some documentary evidence to SFE by post e.g. a birth or marriage certificate, if you don’t have a UK passport. Please respond to all requests from SFE as soon as possible to prevent delays.

For a paper copy of the Part-time Tuition Fee Loan application form please contact on:

Phone: 0300 100 0607
Monday to Friday: 08:00 - 20:00
Saturday and Sunday: 09:00 - 17:30

Minicom 0300 100 0622 (only works with a Minicom device)

Already applied for your loan? Complete your enrolment now (you'll need your CRN)

Use your CRN to complete your enrolment

Attention: When your application is complete and you have your CRN: You're ready to complete your enrolment. To do this we'll ask you for your CRN number as proof that you’ve applied for your Tuition Fee Loan, so make sure you have this to hand before you proceed.

You'll also need to confirm you want to pay all or part of your module fee with your Tuition Fee Loan.

Online applicants: you'll have been given a CRN shortly after setting up your account. If you haven't already done so, please complete your loan application immediately so that it can be processed before your module start.

Complete your enrolment by calling The Open University on 0845 300 6090.

You'll also be able to complete enrolment on any other modules that you've asked us to hold for you. We'll remind you to do this from your StudentHome page.

Note: your records will update overnight, after which we'll display your CRN on this page.
