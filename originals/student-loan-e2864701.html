Your student loan for Certificate of Higher Education Open

You have previously expressed an interest in paying for OU study with a Tuition Fee Loan from Student Finance England (SFE).

When you enrolled on a module, we asked you a number of questions to determine whether you might be eligible to apply for a Tuition Fee Loan. According to your answers you may not be eligible and must offer an alternative payment method.

Student Finance England ultimately decides who is eligible for a Tuition Fee Loan. You can, however, make a retrospective loan application to SFE after your qualification has started and, if approved, you can change your payment method to a loan and receive a refund on your original payment method. The Tuition Fee Loan application is straightforward and only takes around 30 minutes to complete at www.gov.uk/studentfinance.

If your circumstances have changed, and you think that you are now eligible to apply for a Tuition Fee Loan, please call us on 0845 300 60 90.

You can find out more about your payment options in Registering and paying for your studies.
