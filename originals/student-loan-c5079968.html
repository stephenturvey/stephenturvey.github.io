Apply for targeted grants

  1. 1Loan application form To apply for targeted grants you must first complete a loan application form, which we will send to you, even if you don't want a loan
  2. 2PTGFD form Student Finance Wales (SFW) will send you a PTGFD form or you can download one from Student Finance Wales.
  3. 3Send sections 1 and 2 to SFW Complete sections 1 and 2 of the PTGFD form and send them to SFW.
  4. 4Send section 3 to FSO Section 3 of the PTGFD form should be sent to the Open University Financial Support Office (FSO) with a covering letter with your name and Personal Identifier (PI) so that we can identify you.
