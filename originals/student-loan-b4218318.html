Your student loan for BA/BSc (Honours) Open degree

Your CRN is: 78089024910

According to our records Student Finance England has not authorised the payment of a Part-time Tuition Fee Loan to the Open University to cover your tuition fees for this academic year. You are currently registered with the Open University and you continue to remain liable for your tuition fees. You can review your Conditions of Registration.
