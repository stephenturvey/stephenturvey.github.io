Why do we need your profile up-to-date?

Online appearance

These settings enable you to personalise the details about yourself that other students and tutors can see when you contribute to online discussions, blogs and wikis. You can choose to restrict who can view these details, or allow everyone to view them.

The Open University is currently updating its online learning systems, so for a while you may have two profiles: one for modules on the current system and another for those on the new system. Please check that both of your profiles are set up as you require. If you want changes to apply to all your modules, you will need to update both profiles.
