W102 Law: concepts and perspectives


This information applies to CMAs, eTMAs and TMAs.

Missing score

It takes at least 5 working days for assignment scores to be set up on your student record. Therefore your assignment may have been returned to you before your score is showing on this site.

If you have not yet received a score for an eTMA or TMA you should contact your tutor in the first instance to check that the assignment has been marked and sent to the University.

Contact us

If your tutor has sent the marked TMA to The Open University but the score is still missing, or if you think your assignment score has been recorded incorrectly, please contact us.
