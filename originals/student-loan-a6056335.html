Your student loan for BA/BSc (Honours) Open degree

Your CRN is: 78089024910

You've applied to Student Finance England (SFE) for a Tuition Fee Loan, so you'll be able to start studying even if your first module starts before your loan has been confirmed.

Next steps:

  1. 1What now? If you haven’t already, apply for your loan now!
  2. 2Within 6-8 weeks You’ll be sent a student entitlement pack
  3. 3Make sure Sign and return your Student Finance Declaration form to SFE
  4. 4Then SFE will confirm to the OU that your loan is approved
  5. 5Finally Sign the OU terms and conditions of your module
  6. 6Start studying Welcome to the OU!
