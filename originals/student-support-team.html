Student Support Team role

Your Student Support Team works with tutors to provide information, administrative support and specialist advice to students on a wide range of issues including:

  • Getting started with your studies e.g. how to prepare and gain confidence.
  • Improving your learning skills e.g. note taking, time management, preparing assignments.
  • Tutorial arrangements e.g. attending a different venue.
  • Your study progress e.g. how to catch up if you fall behind.
  • Disability, health problems or learning difficulties affecting your studies.
  • Revision, examinations & ECA queries e.g. examination arrangements, revision planning.
  • Planning a programme of study e.g. module & qualification planning.
  • Careers information, advice and guidance e.g. academic references, careers interviews.
  • Studying with English as an additional language.

Your tutor is your first point of contact for any queries directly related to the content of your module, including assignments and tutorials.
