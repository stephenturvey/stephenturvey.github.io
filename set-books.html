Set books information

AA100 The arts past and present

You need to buy the set books listed below, which form an essential part of your module.

Gurinder Chadha (Director) Bhaji on the Beach (Channel Four DVD)     £15.99
Note: This DVD can be bought for £10.99 delivered direct from the Channel4Shop at www.play.com. It is also available from internet and high street retailers
Heaney, Seamus (tr) The Burial at Thebes 9780571223626 Faber and Faber £9.99
Marlowe, Christopher: O'Conor, John (ed) Doctor Faustus the A text 9780582817807 Longman £7.99
Muldoon, Paul (ed) The Faber Book of Beasts 9780571195473 Faber and Faber £9.99
Prescott, Lynda (ed) A World of Difference: an anthology of short stories from five continents 9780230202085 Palgrave Macmillan £11.99

The Open University does not sell set books. Set books are available to purchase or order, from all bookshops and online booksellers. To find bookshops in your area you can search The Booksellers Association.

The following online booksellers offer discounts:

  • Waterstones
  • Amazon
  • VitalSource - eBooks only

Always quote the ISBN (International Standard Book Number) when purchasing books to ensure that you get the correct edition.

Please note that the recommended retail price is given as a guide only and prices may vary.

If you have difficulty finding a title please contact the Set Book Scheme at The Open University on telephone +44 (0) 1908 653515 (out of office hours please leave a message) or send us an email.
