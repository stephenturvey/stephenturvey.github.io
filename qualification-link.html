Change the modules counted towards

Bachelor of Engineering (Honours)

Click a box to count another module. Where you have already planned on counting a module, the module is shown below with a tick. If you wish to stop counting a module, click the box to clear it.

Note: You must ensure that at least one-third of the credits required for this qualification are from OU or approved collaborative scheme courses that you have not counted in any other OU qualification. Credit counted in undergraduate level qualifications of less than 120 credits will be treated as uncounted credit for this purpose towards any bachelors degree. We recommend that you carefully check the regulations for the qualification. These regulations will be used to determine if you can claim the qualification when you complete your study. If you need advice contact your Regional or National Centre.

Please tell us your new study goal if you no longer wish to study for this qualification.
