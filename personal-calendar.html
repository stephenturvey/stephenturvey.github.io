Personal Calendar

The Open University is currently updating its online learning systems, so for a while you may have two calendars, one for modules on the current system and another for modules on the new system.

Please note that certain new features, such as the ability to display personal events on your module study planner, are only available on the new system. Also, there is no synchronization between the two calendars, so if you would like personal events to display in both calendars, you should enter them in both calendars.

If you choose to export your OU personal calendar to another electronic calendar such as the Google calendar, it is necessary to export both calendars to see all your module events on the Google calendar, and only personal events entered on the new calendar can be exported in this way.
